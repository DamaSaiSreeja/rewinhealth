Technologies
we offer

We deliver personalized evidence-based digital therapeutics using these cutting edge technologies

1. Patient App

Allows the patients to complete assessment, set goals and do therapy all along working with the therapists.

Rehab: Stroke & Musculoskeletal
Features: Personalised, Functional & ADL Goal setting, Progress tracking, Chat & Video Support.
Stroke: Mild to Moderate stroke patient
MSK: Prevention, Acute and Chronic pain
Benefits: Anytime and Anywhere therapy, Local language support, and Low bandwidth app

2. Therapist App
Specifically designed to enable therapists to ensure continuum of care by engaging with patients living at home.

Features: E-assessments, Therapeutic exercise library, Progress tracking, and E-Reports
Benefits: On demand patient usage tracking, Accurate motion tracking, Simple and Easy implementation, and Multi patient support.
3. Motion Sensor Devices
By creating a way to digitize the patient’s motion with clinical precision using Motion Sensors, we have unlocked the potential to automate the Rehab Therapist role

Coverage: Every body part (Shoulder, Elbow, Wrist, Hip, Knee, Ankle, Back & more)
Features: 300+ Real time Incorrect movement Feedback & ROM Tracking for Patient & Therapist
Benefits: Real time monitoring for Therapist, Virtual Assistance at home assistance and Improved
4.Virtual Reality
Therapeutic and fun games that addresses a wide variety of active ROM exercises focused on ADL activities and cognitive functions.

Coverage: Therapeutic, Balance, Cognition, & Pain Management VR therapeutic games
Features: Customise & control sessions and Patient progress tracking.
Target Areas: Stroke & MSK: Upper extremity and hand dysfunction
Benefits: Higher therapy dosage, Engaging & High patient motivation, better outcome

How does it work?

Here’s how it works in 3 easy steps after the therapist enrols the patients.

1. Patients complete the assessment based on various global standards.

2. Therapists set functional goals and design the therapy program using the in-built exercise options.

3. Patients follow the daily therapy calendar with the progress monitored continuously
